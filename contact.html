Setup instructions (remove this block before publishing)

  1. Go to formspree.io and create a free account. Create a new form and copy your form ID (looks like xpwzabcd).
  2. In the <form> tag below, replace YOUR_FORMSPREE_ID with your actual form ID. Formspree will email every submission to the address you sign up with — use your GoDaddy email.
  3. For Google Sheets logging: in Google Sheets, go to Extensions → Apps Script. Paste the script from README.md (included in your download). Deploy it as a web app, copy the URL, and replace YOUR_GOOGLE_SCRIPT_URL below.
  4. Test the form, then delete this pink box before going live.

Both Formspree (free tier: 50 submissions/month) and Google Sheets logging are completely free to start.
