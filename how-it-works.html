The process

Home management that actually knows your home.

Here's exactly what happens from the moment you sign up — no guesswork, no surprises.

Step by step

Five steps to a household that runs itself.

Most home management tools give you a blank template and wish you luck. We do something different.

Start today
1

Pick your plan

Choose Starter, Essential, or Premium based on how much support you need. Not sure? Most moms start with Essential — it covers the most ground and includes a personal onboarding call.

You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. No long commitments.
2

Complete your family profile

After signing up, you'll fill out a short intake form. We ask about your kids' ages and activities, any allergies or dietary restrictions, food preferences, your grocery budget, and the nights each week that are absolute chaos.

This is what makes everything personal. No two family profiles are the same — and no two deliverables will be either.
3

Choose your services

Select the services that matter most to your household — meal planning, scheduling, cleaning systems, kids' school organization, or any combination. Starter members pick 3, Essential picks 6, and Premium gets everything.

4

Receive your deliverables

Based on your profile and service selections, we build everything from scratch — personalized, ready to use, and delivered straight to your inbox. No blank templates to figure out yourself.

Starter: within 5 business days. Essential: within 3. Premium: within 2.
5

We check in and adjust

Life changes — kids switch sports, picky eaters evolve, schedules shift. Essential and Premium members get monthly check-ins so your support always reflects your real life.

Common questions

Good questions deserve honest answers.

How long does it take to get my first deliverables?
Starter members receive their first deliverables within 5 business days of completing their profile. Essential within 3 business days, Premium within 2.
Can I change which services I've selected?
Yes — once per billing cycle. Just reach out before your renewal date and we'll update your selections for the following month.
What if my family situation changes mid-month?
That's exactly what the check-ins are for. Let us know at any time and we'll update your profile. Essential and Premium members can request an interim update outside the monthly check-in too.
Do I need to subscribe to buy from the shop?
Not at all. The shop is open to everyone. All templates are one-time purchases with instant download — no account required.
Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes, anytime. Cancel before your next billing date and you won't be charged. No long contracts, no cancellation fees.
