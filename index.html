The problem

"You're not disorganized. You're just carrying a mental load that nobody else can see."

Most moms aren't struggling because they lack willpower or a good planner. They're struggling because they're the only one who holds everything — the allergies, the sports schedules, the grocery rotation, the school deadlines, the chore chart nobody follows.
