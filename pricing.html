Questions

Straightforward answers.

Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel before your next billing date and you won't be charged. No cancellation fees, no awkward calls.
Can I upgrade or downgrade?
Upgrade anytime — your new tier kicks in immediately. Downgrades apply at the start of your next billing cycle.
Is there an annual discount?
Yes — annual subscribers save the equivalent of two months. Reach out to ask about annual pricing.
What payment methods do you accept?
All major credit and debit cards via Stripe. Billing is automatic on your monthly renewal date.
Can I change my selected services?
Once per billing cycle. Just reach out before your renewal date and we'll update your services for the following month.
Not sure which plan to pick?
Most moms start with Essential — it covers the most ground and includes the onboarding call that makes everything click. You can always start with Starter and move up.
